About the project

Our aim is to bring people together in an interactive online space to foster community connection, and to give them a tool to archive and share the stories that they care about. We hope our tool will increase visibility and amplify the voices of Philly residents in light of recent neighborhood changes while also acting as a living archive of the history of Phillyâ€™s neighborhoods. Who lives there? Who used to live there and what are their stories? How has the neighborhood changed from 20 years ago? Has it been good change?



Our goal is to
Inspire different neighborhoods with stories of resilience and innovation
Create self sustained community storytelling platform
Connect with people at the source i.e. churches, schools, public gathering spaces
Share stories for connecting, advocacy, awareness and celebration
Focusing on stories themselves and themes within communities without added logins or accounts
