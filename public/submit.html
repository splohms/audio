How to submit



1. (optional) Choose a prompt you want to respond to in your story.
2. Give your story a title
3. (optional) You can tell your story through many ways, none of which are mandatory. You can submit text under the "What's your story" promt, and/or upload an image, and/or a voice recording.
4. (optional) You can either take a photo through the website using the "Take photo" button, or by clicking "Browse files" to upload from your device.
5. (optional) Feel free to talk to us! In order to record a story, first click the "Record" button. You will have to give the site permissions, then the recording starts automatically. You can re-do voicenotes if you want, and once you're happy with your recording you can move on to submitting the post.
6. Hit the submit button once you're done, and your story should immediately appear on the homepage :)
